# DNS Setup Strategy

## Apex Domain (`sohamtrading.com`)
- **Type:** ALIAS (or ANAME)
- **Target:** `soham-trading.netlify.app.`
- **Note:** Never use an A Record pointing to a static IP for Netlify, as their edge nodes dynamically scale. If your registrar does not support ALIAS records, you MUST migrate DNS to Cloudflare or Netlify DNS.

## WWW Subdomain (`www.sohamtrading.com`)
- **Type:** CNAME
- **Target:** `soham-trading.netlify.app.`

## Email Security Records (Mandatory)
- **SPF:** `TXT @ v=spf1 include:_spf.google.com ~all` (Adjust based on your email workspace provider).
- **DKIM:** Generated by your email provider.
- **DMARC:** `TXT _dmarc v=DMARC1; p=reject; rua=mailto:security@sohamtrading.com;`
